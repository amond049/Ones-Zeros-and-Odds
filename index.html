Welcome to Ones, Zeros & Odds!

What is Ones, Zeros & Odds?

Ones, Zeros & Odds is a game where the player can truly test their luck with a coin. The player works their way from right to left flipping a coin at each binary position to determine whether or not the number becomes a 1 or 0.




How to play?

You need to pay $1.00 in order to play (of course I have no way of enforcing this, don't tell anyone!) and then you can go ham with flipping the coin. If your final number is less than 140, you get no money back! If your final value is between 140 and 174 (inclusive), you get your initial fee, and if you get 175 or above, you struck the jackpot, you get twice your money back!

The red square above each number is meant to represent the position you are flipping the coin for!
